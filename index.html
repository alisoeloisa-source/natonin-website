Tourism Spots in the Mountainous Terrain of Natonin

Natonin is a municipality located in Mountain Province. It is known for its stunning rice terraces, waterfalls, lakes, and mountain landscapes. It is ideal for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Pénadna Falls

A tall waterfall located in Alunogan, Natonin. It is beside the road and is popular for picture-taking.

Te-chag mad-an Falls

Located in Tonglayan, Natonin. It offers a scenic view and requires a 20–30 minute hike.

Apatan Rice Terraces

Found in Barangay Butac, below Mt. To’or. Handcrafted by early Balangao people.

Mt. To’or

A spot for sightseeing, hiking, and relaxation with panoramic views.

Sakaraw Camping Site

Located in Barangay Poblacion. A peaceful camping and sightseeing area.

Lake Hafiyen

A green lake in Barangay Balangao, accessible via a 2 km hike.
